Prepay for Excess Luggage

Make the most of our prepaid baggage offer to escape last-minute airport dashes! Any additional weight carried by guests beyond the allotted Free Baggage Allowance (FBA) is considered excess baggage. Our prepaid baggage offer allows you to pay for excess baggage ahead of time (up to 6 hours before the flight departure) and save up to *20% from the charges at the airport. Guests holding a valid e-ticket starting with the number 098 can use this offer for all Air India flights (except interline and codeshare flights).
